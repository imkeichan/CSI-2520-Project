Undercover

Undercover (sometimes called Undercoverism) is a Japanese streetwear clothing brand founded by Jun Takahashi. The brand has been called "the essence of Japanese cool" and features finely crafted clothing pieces which are, in the words of Takahashi himself, "strange, but beautiful".
